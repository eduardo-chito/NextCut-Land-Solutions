Paver Patios & Walkways

Pavers are a great way to add a clean, high-end look without pouring concrete. We prep a solid base, install pavers with proper leveling, and finish with edging so everything stays locked in place.

Great for: patios, walkways, fire pit areas, and clean transitions from driveway to entry.

What matters most: base prep and drainage. A strong base prevents sinking, shifting, and puddles.

Gravel Pads

Need a stable surface for a shed, parking area, equipment, or a clean driveway extension? Gravel pads are durable, cost-effective, and look great when installed correctly.

We handle: leveling, base fabric (when needed), gravel selection, and compacting for a firm finish.

Optional upgrades: border edging, drainage tie-ins, and widened access for trailers.

Edging & Borders

Edging gives a clean, finished look and helps keep gravel, rock, or mulch contained. We install durable borders that hold shape and improve the overall appearance of your outdoor areas.

Common options: stone borders, paver edging, and clean separation between beds and lawn areas.

Retaining-Style Borders (Light Wall Work)

For small elevation changes, garden edges, or defined areas, a low retaining-style border can prevent erosion and make your landscape look organized. We can build clean, practical borders for small grade changes.

Good fit for: garden beds, rock areas, sloped edges, and tidy property lines.

Drainage Improvements

Water problems ruin outdoor projects fast. If water is pooling near a patio, washing out gravel, or cutting ruts in your yard/driveway, we can improve drainage so your hardscaping lasts.

Examples: re-directing runoff, adding gravel drainage paths, correcting low spots, and improving flow near driveways and walkways.
