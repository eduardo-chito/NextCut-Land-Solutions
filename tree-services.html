Tree Removal

If a tree is dead, leaning, storm-damaged, or too close to a home, fence, or driveway, removal may be the safest option. We evaluate the tree’s condition, fall direction, and surrounding risk (structures, power lines, tight access), then remove it with a plan that prioritizes safety and property protection.

Common reasons for removal: dead/dying trees, split trunks, root damage, storm hazards, trees rubbing on roofs, and trees blocking new builds or lot prep.

What’s included (typical): on-site evaluation, controlled removal, cutting/stacking, and optional haul-off. Stump grinding is available after removal.

Tree Trimming

Trimming improves safety, appearance, and tree health when done correctly. Whether you need clearance off a roof, driveway, or fence line — or you want to reduce risk before storm season — we trim with a clean, intentional approach that avoids unnecessary damage.

Trim services include: limb removal for clearance, crown thinning (reduce wind load), deadwood removal, shaping, and raising the canopy for access/visibility.

Good to know: We’ll talk through what you want the finished look to be (clean & natural vs heavy cut), and we’ll recommend the safest approach based on the tree type and placement.

Stump Grinding

Stumps are more than an eyesore — they can be a tripping hazard, attract pests, and get in the way of mowing, fencing, landscaping, or new construction. Stump grinding removes the stump below ground level so you can reclaim the space.

Ideal for: yards, fence lines, driveways, building pads, and anywhere you want a clean finish.

What to expect: We grind the stump down, and you’ll be left with grindings (wood chips). We can spread them on-site or haul them off if requested.

Cleanup / Haul-Off

After tree work (or storm damage), debris piles can quickly become a headache. We offer cleanup and haul-off to remove limbs, brush, logs, and leftover material so your property looks clean and usable again.

Cleanup options: brush/limb removal, log removal, pile breakdown, loading and haul-off, and final “jobsite ready” cleanup.

Want it extra clean? Ask about bundling cleanup with trimming/removal so everything is handled in one visit.
